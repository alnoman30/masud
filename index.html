Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my ad creative design services and process.

What platforms do you create ad creatives for?

I create ad creatives for all major platforms including Facebook, Instagram, Google Display Network, YouTube, LinkedIn, Twitter, TikTok, and Pinterest. Each platform has unique specifications and best practices, and I optimize designs accordingly.

How long does it take to create ad creatives?

Typical turnaround time is 3-5 business days for static ad creatives and 5-7 business days for animated or video ads. Rush delivery is available for urgent campaigns. The timeline also depends on the number of variations and complexity of the project.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes, I include 2 rounds of revisions with every project to ensure the final creatives meet your expectations. Additional revisions can be accommodated if needed. I believe in collaborative refinement to achieve the best results.

What file formats do you deliver?

I deliver ads in all required formats for your platforms: JPG, PNG (with transparency if needed), MP4 for video ads, and GIF for animated creatives. All files are optimized for web and meet platform specifications for size and dimensions.

Can you help with ad strategy and copywriting?

Absolutely! I offer creative strategy services including concept development, A/B testing recommendations, and ad copywriting. I can help craft compelling headlines, body copy, and CTAs that complement the visual design and drive conversions.

What information do you need to get started?

To begin, I need your brand guidelines (logo, colors, fonts), target audience information, campaign objectives, key messaging points, any product images or assets, and platform specifications. The more details you provide, the better I can tailor the creatives to your needs.
